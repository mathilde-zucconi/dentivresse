What is Dentivresse ?

Dentivresse offers you the equivalent of a breathalyser with all the advantages. It is a patch that attaches to the tooth, is smaller and can stay attached all evening, so it doesn't clutter up your bag. It is reusable, so it's more environmentally friendly. It is connected to your phone via an application that provides a wealth of information like knowing your blood alcohol level in real time, so it is more modern. Finally, it lets you combine the practical with the aesthetic, since we have different modern designs.
